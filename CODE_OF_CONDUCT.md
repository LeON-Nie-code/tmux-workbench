# Code of Conduct

This project follows a simple standard: be respectful, direct, and constructive.

Examples of expected behavior:

- Focus criticism on the code, design, or behavior.
- Assume good intent when asking for clarification.
- Keep issues and pull requests actionable.

Examples of unacceptable behavior:

- Harassment, threats, or personal attacks.
- Publishing private information.
- Repeated low-signal or off-topic comments after maintainers ask you to stop.

Maintainers may remove comments, close issues, or block contributors who do not
follow these expectations.
